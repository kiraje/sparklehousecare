Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

help How does steam cleaning actually work?

Our industrial-grade steam machines heat water to 200°F, producing highly pressurized steam. This steam physically breaks down molecular bonds in stains, dissolves grease, kills bacteria on contact, and extracts allergens immediately without relying on harsh chemical soaking.

help Is the cleaning process safe for kids and pets?

Absolutely. Because the core of our cleaning power comes from high-temperature water vapor rather than chemical agents, there's no toxic residue left behind. Any solutions we do use are 100% plant-based and citrus-derived, making your home totally safe for your family and furry companions.

help How long does it take for things to dry?

Because our professional extractors vacuum up over 90% of the moisture used during the steam process, drying times are dramatically reduced compared to traditional shampooing. Most sofas and carpets are completely dry and ready for normal use within 2 to 4 hours.
