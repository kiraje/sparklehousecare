What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and understand how visitors use different pages.

Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose
_ga, _ga_* Analytics Google Analytics 4 — helps us understand how many people visit the site and which pages are most popular.
sparklehousecare_pref Functional Remembers your dark/light mode preference across visits.

How to Manage or Opt Out

You can control cookies in several ways:

  • Browser settings: Most browsers let you block or delete cookies. See your browser's help documentation for instructions.
  • Google Analytics opt-out: Download the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

Disabling certain cookies may affect some features of this website.
